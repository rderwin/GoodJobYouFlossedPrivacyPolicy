Need help with your flossing habit tracker?

How to Use the App

Logging Today's Floss:
Tap the main button to mark that you flossed today and build your streak!

Editing Your Streak:
Go to the calendar page to edit any past days. Tap on any date to add or remove a floss entry for that day.

Viewing Your Progress:
The calendar shows your flossing history at a glance. Green days = you flossed, empty days = missed days.

Troubleshooting

App not responding?
Try force-closing and reopening the app, or restart your device.

Lost your streak?
Use the calendar page to edit and restore your previous entries.
